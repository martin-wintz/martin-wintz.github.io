Hi. I’m Martin Wintz.

I’m an artist, teacher, writer, and coder passionate about creating and teaching story-driven illustration, as well as experimenting with and building digital tools as part of my process. My passion is sequential art such as comics and graphic novels — the most comprehensive form of visual storytelling that an artist can manage on their own — as well as the intersection of technology and illustration.

I started my artistic journey 4 years ago after working as a software engineer for almost a decade, including at Amazon AWS. I am mostly self-taught, but also took courses at CGMA and studied under the mentorship of renowned Spanish comic illustrator Miki Montllo. I’m currently focused on teaching and experimenting with bringing more technology into my workflow using real-time 3d engines, virtual reality, and artificial intelligence.
